List Of Fascinating Data Insights

  • Space Exploration
    • There are over 100 billion galaxies in the observable universe.
    • NASA's Voyager 1 spacecraft, launched in 1977, is the farthest human-made object from Earth (over 14 billion miles away!).
    • One teaspoon of a neutron star would weigh about 6 billion tons!
  • Human Body
    • The human body contains around 37.2 trillion cells.
    • Your stomach gets a new lining every 3 to 4 days to protect itself from acid.
    • Humans share about 60% of their DNA with bananas.
  • Internet Statistics
    • Over 4.9 billion people worldwide are internet users.
    • There are over 300 hours of video uploaded to YouTube every minute.
    • The first website ever created, created by Tim Berners-Lee in 1991, is still online today!
  • Animals
    • Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood!
    • A group of flamingos is called a "flamboyance."
    • Honey never spoils! Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still edible.
  • Technology Innovations
    • The first computer virus, called "Creeper," was created in the early 1970s.
    • In 2023, more than 13% of the world's population used AI-powered devices regularly.
    • There are currently more than 1 billion smartphones in use worldwide.
